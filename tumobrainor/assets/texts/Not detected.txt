To prevent brain tumours, you should:
1. Sufficient sleep and rest, observance of the daily regime
2. refusal of alcohol, tobacco, drugs
3. Adherence to a healthy diet and refusal of smoked meat
4. Reducing the continuous use of mobile phones, computers and other electronic devices
5. Avoiding stressful situations and overwork

Despite this, it is impossible to completely protect yourself from the appearance of neoplasms in the brain, so it is necessary to periodically undergo a medical examination and various methods of diagnostics of the brain (MRI, CT)
